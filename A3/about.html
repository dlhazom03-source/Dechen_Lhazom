Clinic Overview

For more than three decades, 100SMILES Dental Care has been part of the local community at 33 Fantail Street in Razole. Our commitment has always been to provide friendly, reliable and compassionate care for patients of all ages.

Our dentists and support team bring more than fifty years of combined experience to the clinic. We offer a full range of dental services that include routine check ups, cleanings, orthodontic treatment, dental implants, cosmetic dentistry and restorative care. We are fully equipped to diagnose and treat common dental concerns using current techniques and modern technology.

Patient education is an important part of the way we work. We aim to give every individual the information they need to understand their oral health and to make confident and informed decisions about their care. Whether you are visiting for preventive treatment, a detailed examination or long term dental planning, our team is dedicated to providing a comfortable environment and a high standard of care.

Mission Statement

Our mission at 100SMILES Dental Care is to provide honest, consistent, and high-quality dental care for every patient. We are committed to creating a calm and respectful environment where people feel listened to and cared for.
