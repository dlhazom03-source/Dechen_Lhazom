About our clinic

Quality care that puts your comfort first.

At 100SMILES Dental Care, we want every patient to feel comfortable, understood and confident in their treatment. With over thirty years serving the Razole community and more than fifty years of combined experience, our team provides trusted general, cosmetic and restorative dentistry in a calm, welcoming setting.

We believe in clear communication and personalised care, taking time to explain your options so you can make informed decisions about your oral health. Using digital X-rays, intraoral cameras, laser procedures and simple online booking, we aim to make every visit smooth, reassuring and centred on your wellbeing.
