100Smiles Dental Care Services

Services For All Your Dental Needs

At 100SMILES Dental Care, we offer a full range of treatments designed to keep your teeth and gums healthy. Whether you need a routine check-up or specialised treatment, our experienced team provides various dental services using proven methods and modern equipment.

General Dentistry: Regular check-ups, cleanings, and fillings to maintain healthy teeth and gums.

Orthodontics: Braces and aligners for improved alignment and bite correction for all age groups.

Cosmetic Dentistry: Whitening, veneers, and other aesthetic treatments that restore natural appearance.

Dental Implants: Durable replacements for missing teeth using advanced implant technology.

Emergency Dental Care: Same-day attention for dental pain, accidents, or unexpected oral issues.

Annual Dental Check-Up Package

Our Annual Dental Check-Up is designed to help maintain your oral health with a range of essential services, including:

  • Physical Examination: A full check of your teeth and gums to identify any early signs of concern.
  • Cleanings: Professional removal of plaque and tartar buildup for clean, smooth teeth.
  • X-Rays: Imaging to identify hidden issues early.
  • Dental Health Evaluation: Professional advice on oral hygiene and disease prevention.
  • Nutritional Guidance: Recommendations to support healthy teeth and gums.
  • Laboratory Work (if required): Diagnostic tests tailored to your dental history.

The Importance of Annual Dental Check-Ups

Regular dental visits are essential for maintaining a healthy, confident smile. Annual Dental Check-ups help detect health issues early, prevent disease, and promote lifelong oral health.

1. Early Detection of Health Issues

Routine check-ups allow dentists to identify potential dental or gum problems before they escalate, leading to more effective and affordable treatments.

2. Preventive Care

Regular cleanings and X-rays reduce the risk of cavities and gum disease, contributing to your overall well-being.

3. Tailored Nutritional Guidance

Your dentist can provide personalised advice about your diet to support strong teeth and gums at every stage of life.

4. Holistic Well-being

Good oral health is linked to better general health. Regular check-ups keep your whole body healthier.
